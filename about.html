About DCODE

A passionate team of engineers, designers, and strategists building the digital future — one project at a time.

Innovating Since 2020

DCODE was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and real business needs. Based in Nairobi, Kenya, we serve clients locally and across the African continent and beyond.

We specialise in creating custom web applications, mobile solutions, and enterprise software that drive business growth and enhance user engagement — delivered with speed, quality, and care.

Our approach combines deep technical expertise with creative problem-solving to produce solutions that are both functionally powerful and visually stunning.
